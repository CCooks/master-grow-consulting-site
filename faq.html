Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? You’ll find answers to common inquiries below. If you don’t see your question, feel free to contact us.

Am I allowed to grow cannabis at home in Nevada?

Adults 21 years or older may grow cannabis at home only if they live more than 25 miles from a dispensary. There is a limit of six plants per adult, with a maximum of 12 plants per household. Plants must be grown in a locked, enclosed area and cannot be visible to the public. Always check local ordinances for additional restrictions.

What makes Grow Doctors different from other consultants?

Our Grow Doctors are certified through our comprehensive training program covering botany, environmental control, nutrient management, pest and disease treatment, compliance and customer care. They follow strict standard operating procedures to ensure consistent, high‑quality service.

Do I need to be home during technician visits?

For in‑home services, we recommend that someone be present to grant access and discuss plant care. If you cannot be home, we can make special arrangements with prior consent and clear instructions.

Can you help me source seeds or clones?

Master Grow Consulting does not sell or transport cannabis products. We can refer you to licensed dispensaries or nurseries where you may purchase seeds or clones in compliance with state laws.

Do you offer services outside Nevada?

We currently operate in Nevada. Licensing opportunities for other states are in development. Join our mailing list to stay informed about expansion plans.
